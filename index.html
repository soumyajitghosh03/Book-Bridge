Overview

Basic information


Type: Shadow Library & File Sharing Program Formerly name: BookFinder Founded: 2009 Development Team: Developed by the Russian team Language support: Support 16 languages, Including Chinese Simplified Server distribution: Servers are located all over the world, including the United States, Russia, Germany, Finland, Malaysia, and Luxembourg Storage capacity: more than 220TB

Resource situation


As of 2025. the cumulative number of books included: E-books: 23,000,000 (including multilingual versions) Academic papers: 84,837,000 (covering SCI/journals) Resource type: covering literature, science and technology, medicine, history and other fields, including classic classics, popular novels, and professional academic papers Format support: support PDF and EPUB, MOBI, TXT and other common formats, some formats can also be converted online

Operations


Source of funding: Mainly rely on user donations, usually fundraising in March and September of each year User scale: In June 2020, Book Bridge had about 2.84 million visiting usen, of which 14.76% were from the United States Website Ranking: According to the Alexa Traffic Rank service. In October 2021. Book Bridge ranked 8182nd among global websites
